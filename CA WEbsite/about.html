Our Story

Founders Cube is a private Startup Business Incubation Center located at Mahadevapura, Whitefield Main Road, Bengaluru. Founders Cube is acting as a platform for startups to meet startup entrepreneurs, mentors, advisors, investors, industry experts, representatives from Govt. department and help them to fit in to the requirements of Startup India Action Plan and other Govt

Aids and grants through awareness, proper monitoring and guidance. We are also providing training and capacity building programs, Interaction with government representatives, awareness programs for legal and compliance requirements and other programs based on the industry needs.
